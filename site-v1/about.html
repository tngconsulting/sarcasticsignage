About Patricia

One Woman. All of It.

Sarcastic Signage is a one-woman operation where attitude meets craftsmanship. Run by Patricia out of Ontario, Canada, every product is made by hand — no factories, no mass production, no corporate inspirational quotes about “living, laughing, and loving.”

What started as a hobby turned into a full crafting obsession. Wooden signs, tote bags, pouches, apparel, accessories — if Patricia can put a sarcastic saying on it, she probably already has. Over 200 tote designs and counting. She is a woman with a serious talent for saying what everyone else is thinking — and the craftsmanship to put it on something beautiful.

Whether you need the perfect gift for someone who appreciates blunt honesty, or just want your home decor to finally reflect your real personality, you’re in the right place.

Custom orders are welcome. If you have a saying in mind, Patricia can probably make it happen.

“I don’t do inspirational. I do relatable.”
— Patricia, Chief Sarcasm Officer

If it doesn’t make you laugh, snort, or nod knowingly — it doesn’t leave the studio.
