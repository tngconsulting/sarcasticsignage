The Story

Run by Patricia in Ontario, Canada, Sarcastic Signage is where attitude meets craftsmanship. Every product is handmade with love — and a heavy dose of sarcasm. Whether you need the perfect gift for someone who appreciates blunt honesty, or just want your home decor to finally reflect your real personality, you're in the right place.
