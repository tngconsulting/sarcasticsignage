The Story

Crafted in Ontario.
Filtered by Nobody.

Sarcastic Signage is a one-woman operation run by Patricia out of Ontario, Canada. Every product is made by hand — no factories, no mass production, no corporate inspirational quotes about "living, laughing, and loving."

What started as a hobby turned into a full crafting obsession. Wooden signs, tote bags, pouches, apparel, accessories — if Patricia can put a sarcastic saying on it, she probably already has. Over 200 tote designs and counting.

"I don't do inspirational. I do relatable."
Patricia, Chief Sarcasm Officer
