# SARCASTIC SIGNAGE — Master Documentation
## Design Brief & Brand Guidelines for Website Rebuild

**Project:** Modernize sarcasticsignage.com from 1990s aesthetic to contemporary, user-friendly, visually appealing design while maintaining brand authenticity and improving UX.

---

## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Business:** Sarcastic Signage is a one-woman operation run by **Patricia** (Chief Sarcasm Officer) creating handmade wooden signs, tote bags, pouches, apparel, and accessories. The brand explicitly rejects corporate inspirational tropes in favor of relatable, unfiltered humor.

**Location:** Handmade in **Ontario, Canada** (Ottawa-based).

**Primary Sales Model:** In-person at local events and markets; secondary sales via phone/text/email for local pickup or domestic shipping.

**Core Promise:** Handmade goods with personality. Every piece is one-of-a-kind. Products must make you "laugh, snort, or nod knowingly" before they leave the studio.

### 2.2 Brand Tone & Voice Principles

- **Brutally honest, relatable, anti-inspirational**
- **Zero filter** — no corporate tropes ("Live, Laugh, Love")
- **Handmade authenticity** — emphasizes handcrafted, one-of-a-kind nature
- **Direct, humorous, unfiltered** — "Delivered with attitude"
- **Personality-driven** — every product has a personality
- **Customer-centric wordplay** — speaks directly to customer values

### 2.3 Visual Direction

**Logo:**
- Horizontal gradient: Deep Plum → Magenta → Red-Orange → Terracotta → Mossy Green
- Heavy, organic watercolor/dry-brush texture
- Jagged, white negative space "torn-paper" effect at bottom edge
- Typography: "SARCASTIC" (light/thin weight) stacked above "SIGNAGE" (heavy/bold weight), white sans-serif

**Color Palette:**
- Deep Plum, Magenta, Red-Orange, Terracotta, Mossy Green (from logo)
- White/Off-white for contrast and negative space
- Consider warm, organic earth tones throughout design

**Typography:**
- Clean, modern sans-serif (mirrors logo aesthetic)
- Contrast light and heavy weights for visual hierarchy
- Handmade aesthetic without being cutesy or overly decorative

**Imagery & Photography Style:**
- Authentic workshop environment (emphasizes handmade, not mass-produced)
- Natural lighting, organic composition
- Products in-context (signs on walls, totes in use, etc.)
- NO photos of Patricia (privacy requirement)
- Workshop/maker aesthetic reinforces "handmade" positioning

## 8. STRATEGIC CONTEXT & KEY DECISIONS

### 8.1 Sales Model Shift

**Decision:** Prioritize **in-person sales** as primary conversion goal.

**Reasoning:**
- Majority of Sarcastic Signage products are actually sold at local markets and events
- Direct contact model allows for customer relationships and customization
- Reflects how the business actually operates
- Removes friction of "click and forget" e-commerce for handmade, unique products

### 8.2 Manual Order Flow

**Decision:** No automated e-commerce checkout; direct contact via call/text/email.

**Reasoning:**
- Each item is handmade and unique — not a traditional inventory system
- Patricia manages custom requests and can discuss options directly
- Maintains personal relationship and brand authenticity
- Aligns with "zero filter" and "delivered with attitude" positioning

### 8.3 Anti-Corporate Aesthetic

**Decision:** Explicitly reject corporate inspirational tropes.

**Reasoning:**
- Differentiates from mass-produced, generic signage
- "Live, Laugh, Love" is the antithesis of Sarcastic Signage brand
- Reinforces handmade authenticity and relatable humor
- Appeals to target audience (adults seeking unfiltered personality)

### 8.4 Geographic Focus: Ontario/Canada Only

**Decision:** Restrict shipping to Canada; no international shipping.

**Reasoning:**
- Simplifies logistics for one-woman operation
- Reinforces local maker identity ("Handmade in Ontario")
- Reduces operational complexity
- Allows focus on quality over scale

### 8.5 Direct Founder Visibility

**Decision:** Feature Patricia as "Chief Sarcasm Officer" and founder, but maintain privacy (no personal photos).

**Reasoning:**
- Anchors brand to real person, building trust
- Explains unique voice and quality standards
- Humanizes one-woman operation
- Privacy-protective: can discuss her role without exposing her personal life/home
